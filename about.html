About Us

With a vision and mission to have Happy Empowered Children in slums, weCare Foundation reaches out to children in urban slums, villages, and on construction sites and changes their lives through education and skills.


We work in the largest state of India - Uttar Pradesh. The issue that we are trying to address is the removal of abject poverty in slums through education and skilling of children.


We become a part of the community by setting up permanent Yellow Rooms and create a sustainable transformational environment for all the children in that vicinity. We work for them to become happy, gender-equal, empathetic, educated, sensitive, and ethical human beings.



There are 3 board members with us. We are a team of 30 full-time employees and around 40 volunteers and interns. We have an Advisory Board of 5 experienced members. The Founder has quit his corporate and academic career and has been working in a full-time capacity in an honorary position. We have a fantastic strength of more than 300 lovely donors who have been taking this cause ahead.

We at weCare charity foundation do our bit in bridging the gap between the privileged and unprivileged. From organizing evening free classes to managing the school and other needs of these kids who are displaced or their social status doesn't allow them to get that. We collaborate with other NGOs, Government officials, and many young and old privileged people around and organize regular visits to slums.


Great things can't be done alone. This all became a possibility because of the amazing volunteers joining us daily. Join our community and do your bit in helping us in extending our reach and spread smiles on more number of children.
